Colloquium: Fundamental Theorem of Algebra

Last Edit: 11 Feb 2021


This is the slides I prepared for my talk at the UCL Undergraduate Math Colloquium on 11th Feb 2021.


Abstract: This will be a talk on the Fundamental Theorem of Algebra. This theorem shows the existence of complex root of a complex polynomial. Some more, the number of roots is the same as the degree of the polynomial. The Fundamental Theorem of Algebra, although has the term 'Algebra' in it, can be proved via methods from other branches of Mathematics. I will be presenting one Analytic (to be more specific, Complex Analytic) proof and one Topological proof. They will be self-contained and accessible to everyone, but some basic knowledge of Complex Analysis and Algebraic Topology will be helpful.
